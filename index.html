About The Team

Vortiqon Science is an independent aerospace research team from K. N. Toosi University of Technology. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theory and practice by developing impactful, hands-on aerospace projects. We document our entire journey to educate and inspire the next generation of engineers through our public outreach and YouTube channel.
